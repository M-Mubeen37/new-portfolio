🚀 Custom Price Calculator Built on Shopify – No App Required! 🚀

CLIENT:

AbuBakar Ashraf

PROJECT TYPE:

Shopify custom coding

DATE:

15 April, 2024

I recently created a fully custom price calculator for the product page at Floorstreet – without using any third-party apps! 🛠️

This custom solution operates in two dynamic ways:

  • 1️⃣ Area-Based Calculation: Enter the area, and the calculator automatically calculates the number of packs, quantity, and price. It even factors in wastage with a simple toggle button. 🧮
  • 2️⃣ Quantity-Based Calculation: Enter the quantity, and the calculator reverses the math to determine the area, pricing, and more. 🔄

All of this was built from scratch using Liquid, JSON, and JavaScript – demonstrating the flexibility and power of custom Shopify development. If you need custom Shopify solutions like this, feel free to reach out! Let's build something unique together. 🔗
